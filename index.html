About Us

Empowering students since 2006

Our Mission

Pushpawati Institutions is more than just a place of learning ‚Äî it is a mission, a vision, and a promise to uplift students from rural and underserved communities by providing them with skill-based, affordable, and career-focused education. Managed by the Shri J.P. Charitable Trust, our institution has been a ray of hope for students seeking growth, opportunity, and transformation.

Our Story

Founded 19 years ago under the leadership of Mr. Narendra Kumar Sharma, a respected lawyer and social visionary, the trust was created with one clear goal: to make education accessible to the needy and help them build strong, secure futures. Since its inception, Pushpawati Institutions has empowered over 2,000 students, many of whom now hold prestigious positions in government departments and private companies.

Our Academic Strength

We offer a range of certified programs in both technical training (ITI) and healthcare education.

  • ITI Courses: Electrician, Fitter, COPA (Computer Operator and Programming Assistant), Fireman. Approved by N.C.V.T. and successfully run for 18+ years. 10th pass eligible. Ajmer Board's 12th pass equivalency available.
  • Diploma in Ayurveda Nursing & Pharmacy (DANP): 2.5 years, certified by the Government of Rajasthan and affiliated with the Rajasthan Ayush Nursing Council, Jaipur. Open to 10+2 students from any stream. Taught by experienced instructors, including retired government doctors with 30+ years of experience.

Proven Results

At Pushpawati Institutions, our students' success is our greatest achievement. Many have gone on to become:

  • Loco Pilots in Indian Railways
  • Technicians at JVVNL (Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam Ltd)
  • Network Engineers in top companies like Reliance Jio
  • Healthcare assistants in both government and private hospitals

We believe every student, regardless of background, has the potential to thrive with the right guidance and education. Our campus is a space where skills are shaped, confidence is built, and futures are created.
